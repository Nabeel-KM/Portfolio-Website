Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process

How quickly can you implement a CI/CD pipeline?

For standard applications, I can implement a basic CI/CD pipeline within 3-5 business days. More complex implementations with multiple environments and custom requirements may take 1-2 weeks.

What's your approach to security in cloud environments?

I follow a defense-in-depth approach with security implemented at multiple layers: network (VPCs, security groups, NACLs), identity (IAM with least privilege), application (vulnerability scanning), and data (encryption at rest and in transit).

How do you handle documentation?

I provide comprehensive documentation for all implementations, including architecture diagrams, setup instructions, operational procedures, and troubleshooting guides. Documentation is delivered in Markdown format for easy integration with wikis or knowledge bases.

What's your availability for ongoing support?

I offer flexible support options, from ad-hoc hourly consulting to dedicated support packages with guaranteed response times. For critical systems, I can provide emergency support with response times as low as 2 hours.

Do you work with existing infrastructure or only new setups?

I work with both new and existing infrastructure. For existing setups, I begin with an assessment to understand the current state before recommending and implementing improvements.

How do you ensure knowledge transfer to our team?

Knowledge transfer is built into my process through documentation, pair programming sessions, and optional training workshops. I ensure your team understands not just how to use the systems, but why they're designed that way.
